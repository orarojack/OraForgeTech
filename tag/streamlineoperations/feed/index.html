Transparent Resource Outsourcing | Lets talk

Create transparent resource outsourcing contracts with OraforgeTechnologies.

Duration: 1-month Campaign

Overview & Purpose

This campaign aims to attract customers who are looking for outsourcing, resourcing, or plain resourcing contracts. Give confidence and attract customers, who are looking to build a great team through resourcing contracts. 

Campaign Idea: Drive more resourcing contracts to the company through outsourcing.

Clarity in Pricing:

Emphasise that every project quote is fully transparent, with detailed breakdowns of costs (development hours, resources, tools, etc.).

“No unexpected costs or hidden charges. What you see is what you pay.”

Flexible Resourcing:

Offer the ability to scale resources up or down based on client needs, ensuring they only pay for what they use.

“We align our team size to your project requirements, offering you flexibility without compromising on quality.”

End-to-End Development Transparency:

Provide a clear understanding of project timelines, resourcing needs, and cost updates at every stage.

“Full visibility into the development process, so there are no surprises—just results.”

Cost-Effective Expertise:

Highlight expertise in optimising costs through efficient development practices and the use of the latest technology stacks.

“Maximizing your ROI through expert resourcing and efficient development workflows.”

Resourcing Contracts

Customers will have specific criteria, including 

Technical expertise, industry experience, track record, and cultural compatibility. 

Additionally, their due diligence in selecting vendors will encompass previous projects and client feedback.

We need to establish pathways for clear communication and effective collaboration once the resourcing contracts are initiated. It is essential to showcase these pathways to customers beforehand.

How to create successful resource contract relationships?

For a successful resource contract relationship, we need a strong team composition, cultural sensitivity, and effective project management.

Team Composition involves identifying key project roles and balancing the team with both in-house and outsourced members.

Cultural Sensitivity requires acknowledging cultural differences within a global team and fostering collaborative cross-cultural communication.

Effective Project Management entails implementing the right methodologies and tools, such as Agile, Scrum, and Kanban, through platforms like JIRA and Asana.

Giving Confidence for Customers

To build confidence in resourcing contracts, we must address security concerns, quality assurance, and contingency solutions.

Security Concerns: We ensure the protection of clients’ sensitive data and provide assurance regarding the legality and security of the project and its data.

Quality Assurance: We establish a stringent quality assurance process that includes acceptance, integration, and unit testing. Confidence is further reinforced through regular testing and feedback loops involving project stakeholders.

Contingency Solutions: We address all potential customer risks by demonstrating a flexible mindset and being prepared to adapt to unforeseen challenges, thereby ensuring project continuity and reliability.

Creating a long term Resourcing Contracts

Continuous Learning and Innovation

We must implement a continuous learning and improvement process for our company and employees, learning from both successes and failures. Conducting post-project reviews will help us analyse what went well and what needs improvement. Resourcing contracts are beneficial for gaining new insights and fostering innovation through our support for customers, learning from them, and innovating on their behalf.

Creating Long-Term Partnerships

Building long-term partnerships requires time and effort to establish bonds of mutual respect, trust, and shared objectives. Continuously evaluate opportunities for expanding collaboration and strategically explore and suggest possible areas for growth and joint ventures.

Channels:

LinkedIn Ads: Target specific industries (e.g., FinTech, HealthTech) with case studies showing successful project completion using niche technologies.

Landing Page: A dedicated page focusing on the transparent costing message, offering case studies showing cost savings from previous clients.

Webinars/Workshops: Host exclusive online sessions about “Scaling Web & Mobile Development Teams” tailored to industry-specific challenges.

Personalized Email Campaigns: For key decision-makers in targeted industries, showcase your specialized resourcing offerings.

Content Marketing: Write thought leadership pieces on challenges in web and mobile development for niche sectors, demonstrating your team’s ability to solve them.

Testimonials & Case Studies: Showcase clients who’ve benefited from clear pricing and successful project outcomes, particularly in industries with complex requirements.
