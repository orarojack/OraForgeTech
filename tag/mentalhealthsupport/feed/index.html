Embracing the Transformation: 

The field of mental healthcare has undergone a revolutionary transformation with the advent of teleconsultation application. This innovative approach offers patients convenient and accessible therapy options that were previously unavailable.

Telepsychiatry

Telepsychiatry, also known as telepsychology or teletherapy, refers to the delivery of mental health services remotely through video conferencing or other telecommunication technologies. It allows individuals to receive psychiatric evaluations, therapy sessions, and medication management from the comfort of their own homes, overcoming barriers such as distance, transportation, and limited access to mental health providers. Telepsychiatry has gained popularity for its convenience, flexibility, and ability to reach individuals in underserved areas, ultimately improving the accessibility and delivery of mental healthcare.
