The revamped ABHA app represents a major leap forward in digital health management, offering several key improvements:

Enhanced User Interface (UI)

The new UI of the ABHA app is designed with simplicity and ease of use in mind. The updated interface makes it straightforward for users to create and manage their ABHA addresses, access health records, and connect with healthcare facilities. This intuitive design is particularly beneficial for users of all technological proficiencies, ensuring a seamless experience.

Simplified Registration and Record Management

With the latest update, users can now register using their mobile number, email ID, or 14-digit ABHA number. Linking the ABHA address with the 14-digit number allows users to become KYC verified, facilitating access to a range of government health programs. This streamlined registration process enhances the integration of health records across various facilities.

Greater Control Over Health Records

One of the standout features of the updated ABHA app is the enhanced control it offers users over their health records. Users can manage who views their data, specify what information can be accessed, and set the duration of access. The ability to revoke consent further strengthens privacy, giving users complete control over their personal health information.
